About

Discover the best cafés, kitchens, pubs and bars around the University of Sheffield campus.

Diamond Kitchen

While home to the most cutting-edge labs and a sprawling study space, The Diamond is also home to a stylish kitchen to fuel busy students.
Grab a popular 'Grill & Go' burrito, wraps, loaded fries, jacket potatoes or a caffeine kick to start your day off right or keep you going.

Heartspace Café

Find delicious coffee at student-friendly prices, a range of bakes and snacks and fresh hot food in one of the most striking architectural highlights across the University of Sheffield campus.
The Engineering Heartspace can be used as both a social and study area, so grab a drink or a bite with friends and have a chat or get some work done.

Open in Maps

Bar One

Brought to you by the Sheffield Students' Union, Bar One is known for both their enticing food menu and frequent events.
Check out their burgers, loaded fries, wraps & subs, sweet treats and variety of drinks. Once you're done, enjoy one a pub quiz or take to the dancefloor.
